The multiple features
implemented

When designing for accessibility, it's important to consider the following UX/UI features for video and audio content on websites:

Closed Captions: Closed captions are essential for users who are deaf or hard of hearing. They provide a text-based representation of the audio content of a video, enabling users to understand the content even if they cannot hear the audio.

Visual Indicators: Provide visual indicators for events such as starting and stopping a video, as well as any other important audio cues.

Transcripts: Providing transcripts of audio content is useful for users who prefer to read the content instead of listening to it.

Keyboard Accessibility: Ensure that all video and audio controls can be easily navigated with a keyboard, as some users may have difficulty using a mouse or other pointing device.

High Contrast: Use high contrast color schemes for the video and audio player, as well as any associated captions, to make it easier for users with low vision to distinguish them.

Some common questions
were often asked

How long does the design process take for me?

The length of the design process for me can vary depending on several factors, including the complexity of the project, and the time available or given to complete the project. Typically, a design project can take anywhere from a few days to several weeks to complete.

What is my favorite part as a interactive media student?

Two words. Product Design as it involves creating tangible items that people use in their daily lives, and it requires a combination of creativity, problem-solving, and technical skills. One of the exciting aspects of product design is the ability to innovate and create something new that improves people's lives or solves a particular problem. Seeing the end result of a product that you've designed, and the impact it can have on the user, can be incredibly rewarding.

What is the role of typography in graphic design?

Typography plays a crucial role in graphic design as it is the visual representation of written language. Choosing the right typeface, font size, and spacing can greatly impact the readability and overall impact of a design.

How do I improve my design skills?

To improve my design skills, practice regularly by creating a variety of designs, seek feedback from peers and mentors, study design theory and principles, stay up to date on design trends and technologies, and attend design workshops and conferences.

How do I handle design criticism?

Handling design criticism can be challenging, but it is important to remember that feedback is essential for growth and improvement. Be open-minded and receptive to feedback, ask questions to better understand the critique, and use it as an opportunity to learn and improve my skills.

How do I stay current with design trends and developments in the industry?

To stay current with design trends and developments, graphic design students can follow design blogs and publications, attend design conferences and workshops, participate in design communities, and experiment with new software and techniques. It is also important to keep an eye on industry trends and anticipate changes in technology and design practices.
